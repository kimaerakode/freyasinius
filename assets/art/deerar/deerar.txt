DEERAR 
DEERAR is a duo that creates collages of sound and image. The project consists of Freya Sinius and Jonas Götze, who create the works in interaction where neither image is put on sound, nor music is created for film, but where these elements are inseparable. 
Deerar emphasizes new logics, asynchronous rhythms and movements, and creates works that are not finished until they are experienced.
